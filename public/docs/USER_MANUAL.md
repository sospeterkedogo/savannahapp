# Savannah Bar & Grill - User Manual

## Getting Started

### Accessing the Dashboard
1. Navigate to `/staff/login`.
2. Enter your staff credentials.
3. Upon login, you will be redirected to the **Staff Dashboard**.

---

## Staff Operations

### 1. Managing Orders
- Go to the **Orders** section from the dashboard.
- View pending, completed, and cancelled orders.
- Click an order to view full details including customer notes and dining options.
- Use the status toggle to move orders through the workflow.

### 2. Inventory Check-in
- Navigate to the **Inventory** page.
- Review current stock levels and "Low Stock" alerts.
- To add stock:
    1. Click **Check-in Stock**.
    2. Select the item from the dropdown.
    3. Enter the quantity received.
    4. (Optional) Upload a scan of the delivery receipt.
    5. Click **Confirm**.

### 3. Shift Management
- Open the **Shifts** page to see your upcoming schedule.
- Use the "Request Shift" feature if you need to swap or request additional hours.

---

## Admin Operations

### 1. Dashboard Metrics
- Admins can view **System Metrics** at the top of the dashboard.
- **Onboarding Rate:** Percentage of staff who have completed their initial tasks.
- **Stock Health:** Real-time count of items below their minimum threshold.

### 2. Updating the Menu
- Go to **Menu Management**.
- You can edit any item's name, price, or description.
- **Availability Toggle:** Instantly hide items from the customer-facing menu if they are out of stock.

---

## Troubleshooting & Support
- **Login Issues:** Ensure you are using the correct staff email. If forgotten, contact your System Administrator.
- **Sync Errors:** Refresh the page. The app uses real-time sync, but a manual refresh can resolve connection hiccups.
- **Technical Support:** For system-wide issues, contact the Dev team via the `studiohub` support channel.
