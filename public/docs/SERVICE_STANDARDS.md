# Savannah Service Standards

## Our Philosophy
At Savannah, we believe in "Luxury through Simplicity." Every interaction should feel effortless for the guest and meticulous from our side.

## The Savannah Greeting
- Always greet guests within 30 seconds of arrival.
- Maintain eye contact and a warm, professional smile.
- "Welcome to Savannah. Are we joining us for dining or cocktails this evening?"

## Service Workflow
1. **Water Service:** Offered immediately upon seating.
2. **Menu Introduction:** Highlight our wood-fired specialties and signature cocktails.
3. **Order Taking:** Repeat the order back to ensure 100% accuracy.
4. **Check-backs:** Follow up "Two minutes or two bites" after serving.

## Appearance
- Uniforms must be pressed and clean.
- Name tags worn on the left lapel.
- Professional grooming at all times.

## Efficiency
- Table turn-around should be managed gracefully.
- Clear empty glasses and plates promptly, but never rush the guest.
- Anticipate needs before they are asked (e.g., refreshing drinks).
