# Staff Onboarding Checklist

## Day 1: Induction
- [ ] Welcome meeting with Department Head.
- [ ] Tour of the premises (FOH, BOH, Staff Room, Fire Exits).
- [ ] Health & Safety briefing.
- [ ] Uniform collection and grooming standards review.

## Week 1: Training
- [ ] Complete profile setup on the Savannah App.
- [ ] Watch training videos in the Onboarding Tracker.
- [ ] POS (Point of Sale) system training.
- [ ] Menu tasting and knowledge test.
- [ ] Shadowing a senior staff member for 3 full shifts.

## Month 1: Integration
- [ ] First performance review with Duty Manager.
- [ ] Full competence in Savannah Service Standards.
- [ ] Independent management of assigned section.

## Continuous Development
- [ ] Bi-monthly menu updates and training.
- [ ] Quarterly safety refresher.
- [ ] Career progression path discussion.
