# Savannah Health & Safety Policy

## General Safety
- All staff must complete the mandatory Health & Safety induction before their first shift.
- "Clean as you go" is a requirement for all areas.
- Spills must be marked with a yellow sign immediately and cleared promptly.

## Kitchen Safety
- Sharp knives must be stored in designated racks.
- Use protective gloves when handling hot equipment.
- Slip-resistant shoes are mandatory in the BOH area.

## Fire Safety
- Familiarize yourself with all fire exits and assembly points.
- Never block a fire exit or extinguisher.
- In case of fire, alert a manager immediately and follow the evacuation procedure.

## Reporting
- Report all accidents, no matter how small, in the Accident Book located in the staff room.
- Report any faulty equipment to the Duty Manager immediately.
