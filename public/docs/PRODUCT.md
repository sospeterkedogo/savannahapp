# Savannah Bar & Grill - Product Overview

## Introduction
Savannah Bar & Grill is a premium dining and management platform designed to provide a seamless experience for both customers and staff. The application integrates high-end aesthetics with powerful backend management tools.

## Key Features

### For Customers
- **Dynamic Menu Browsing:** Explore our curated menus (Main, Pasta, Steak, Cocktails, etc.) with real-time availability.
- **Online Ordering:** Seamless cart and checkout process for Collection, Table Orders, or Delivery.
- **Table Bookings:** Real-time reservation system with instant confirmation.
- **Event Discovery:** Stay updated with live jazz nights, wine tastings, and seasonal celebrations.

### For Staff
- **Order Management:** Track and process customer orders in real-time.
- **Inventory Tracking:** Advanced stock management with low-stock alerts and easy check-in via receipt scanning.
- **Shift Management:** View schedules and request shifts directly through the dashboard.
- **Personal Storage:** Securely store and manage personal documents (IDs, training certs).

### For Admins
- **System Metrics:** Real-time dashboard showing onboarding rates, stock health, and recent activities.
- **Menu Management:** Instantly update prices, availability, and descriptions.
- **Audit Logging:** Full transparency of system actions for security and accountability.

## Technical Excellence
- **Glassmorphic UI:** Modern, high-end visual language using backdrop filters and luxury accents.
- **Real-time Sync:** Powered by Supabase for instant updates across all devices.
- **Scalable Architecture:** Built with Next.js and TypeScript for performance and reliability.
