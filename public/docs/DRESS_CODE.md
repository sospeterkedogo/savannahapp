# Savannah Staff Dress Code

## General Principles
The Savannah uniform is designed to reflect our high-end, contemporary grill house aesthetic. It is a mix of classic elegance and modern comfort.

## Front of House (FOH)
- **Servers:** Black button-down shirt (long sleeve, rolled to mid-forearm), dark indigo denim or black trousers, leather-soled shoes.
- **Bar:** Black vest over a grey or white crisp shirt, dark apron with luxury brass fittings.
- **Hosts:** Tailored black blazer or dress, subtle luxury accessories.

## Back of House (BOH)
- **Chefs:** Classic white double-breasted jacket, checked or black utility trousers, non-slip professional footwear.
- **Kitchen Porter:** Black branded tee, heavy-duty apron, non-slip footwear.

## Grooming Standards
- Hair should be neatly styled and away from the face.
- Subtle jewellery only (studs, wedding bands).
- Personal hygiene is paramount; light scents only.

## Maintenance
- Uniforms are provided by the company.
- Any damage or wear should be reported to the manager for immediate replacement.
- Laundered uniforms are available at the start of every shift.
